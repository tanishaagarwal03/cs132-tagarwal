Indulge in Edinburgh Food Scenes

Edinburgh is so multicultural. You can find almost any cuisine here. Many Indian restaurants are owned by Indians and are authentic. The Thai and Japanese restaurants are amazing as well! I would also recommend trying the various dessert shops all over the city!

The Milkman

Best Dish: Their selection of specialty coffee paired with the freshest pastries
Why it's good: Perfect for a quick pick-me-up in a cozy setting
Cuisine: Cafe

Haute Dolci

Best Dish: Signature waffles with ice cream and toppings
Why it's good: Perfect spot for dessert lovers with elegant plating
Cuisine: Dessert

Laila Bar and Restaurant

Best Dish: Grilled halloumi with pomegranate seeds
Why it's good: A refreshing blend of Mediterranean flavors
Cuisine: Mediterranean (good for a boozy brunch)

Maki & Ramen

Best Dish: Tonkotsu Ramen
Why it's good: Comforting broth with rich, authentic Japanese flavors
Cuisine: Japanese

Tuk Tuk

Best Dish: Chicken lollipops with masala fries
Why it's good: A fun and vibrant twist on Indian street food
Cuisine: Indian Street Food
