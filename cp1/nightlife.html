Social Scenes in Edinburgh

The legal age for drinking in Scotland is 18. Edinburgh is famous for its diverse social scenes, from casual pubs to lively clubs. Here's a brief guide to some popular venues! Be sure to get on guestlists for cheaper entry!

DROPKICKS

DropKicks is a go-to pub on Fridays for its vibrant atmosphere and student-friendly vibe. You would most certainly find UoE students there almost every night!

WHY NOT

Why Not offers themed nights and a lively atmosphere, perfect for a night out on Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays and Saturdays! It has three rooms with different DJs and themes as well! Don't forget to check if you can get on the guestlist to save some quid.

SUBWAY

Known for its affordable drinks and student crowds, Subway is a favorite for Tuesday nights (free entry!!!!!). If pop music from 2010 is your vibe - Subway Tamagatchi Tuesdays are your go-to. Don't forget to start queueing at 9:30 so you can get stamped!

BOTECO

A bar that turns into a club on Friday nights, Boteco is known for its Latin music and dance vibes, making it a fun Friday destination. If you enjoy international crowds, spicy drinks and vibing to Spanish music, this is your go to!

HIVE

The Hive attracts mostly students from different universities in Edinburgh and is a favourite because it stays open till 5 AM! it is also very famous for its rainbow shots!
