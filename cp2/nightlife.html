Nightlife in Edinburgh

Edinburgh has a vibrant nightlife scene, offering everything from traditional pubs to modern clubs and hidden cocktail bars. Here are some must-visit spots to explore after dark (which happens pretty early in Edinburgh). Remember the legal drinking age is 18 in the UK and you might have to show your government ID.
